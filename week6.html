Tip of the week.

Start doing projects that combined your passion and programming. A couple of years ago, someone asked me "What's your passion for life?" My response was "Cooking and Coding, the double C's!" Then I thought to myself, why not combine those two? So I did. I started a cooking blog, recipes site of all of my favorite dishes and what I thought about it. It started as a small and silly project but now I found myself constantly making posts and I actually got responses from people. So I would advise you to do the same. Find your passions (If you haven't already) and combine them together. You will be surprise of the result! Besides, recruiters always love to see personality and who you are when you're not focusing on work.
