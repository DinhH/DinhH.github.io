Tip of the week.

Tip of the week this week is based on what happened to me during the project, "tunnel vision". I'm sure any of you who had spent hours of coding came across this problem before and I'm sure it was not a pleasant experience. For me, it was helpful to stop and take a break, distract your eyes with something different than coding. Also talking through your code with someone can also bring out the bug. In addition to that, you can always have the rubber duck debugging technique!
