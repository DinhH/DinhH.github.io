Pick of the week.

There are some talk this week about the development of Elon Musk Hyperloop. If you don't already know what it is, hyperloop an idea founded Tesla's founder Elon Musk. He has the vision to move people or things anywhere in the world quickly, safely, efficiently, on-demand and with minimal impact to the environment. When he said "quickly", it means you can travel from California to New York in under an hour. Right now, there has been designs submitted by college students all around the country proving that Elon's idea is actually doable.
