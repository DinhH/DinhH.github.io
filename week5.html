Pick of the week

My pick of the week is the news about Yahoo's security breach. 500 million accounts were stolen back in late 2014. Many of those account information may have included names, email addresses and hashed passwords. In some cases, the encrypted or unencrypted security questions and answers. Some reports criticised Yahoo for not inform the public right away. Many believe that if Yahoo knew about this since early August, they have failed to coordinate with law enforcement. This is not Yahoo first security breach, you would think as one of the world leading sites, protecting user's details would be their number one priority.
